Privacy Policy for Sketch Circle

Last Updated: February 7, 2026 (v2)

1. Introduction

Sketch Circle ("the Game") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Game.

2. Information Collection

2.1 What We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal Information: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (names, emails, addresses, phone numbers, etc.).
  • Server Data: The Game is completely offline. No gameplay data, game state, or user information is transmitted to any external servers.
  • Advertising Data: We do not display advertisements, and therefore do not collect advertising-related information.
  • Tracking Data: We do not use analytics services to track your gameplay or behavior.

2.2 Device Permissions

The Game requires the following device permissions for gameplay purposes:

  • Bluetooth: Used to enable multiplayer connectivity between nearby devices. Connection data is transmitted only between devices on your local network and is not sent to external servers.
  • WiFi: Used to detect available networks for local multiplayer connectivity. WiFi scanning data is processed locally on your device only.
Important: All connectivity data is processed locally on your device and never leaves your device.

3. In-App Purchases

  • The Game offers in-app purchases exclusively for unlocking additional players (up to the Game's maximum player limit).
  • In-app purchase transactions are handled by your device's app store platform (Apple App Store, Google Play Store, etc.) and are subject to that platform's privacy policies.
  • We do not receive or store payment information related to your purchases.
  • Purchase history for additional player unlocks is stored locally on your device only.

4. Data Storage

  • All Game data is stored locally on your device.
  • We do not maintain servers, databases, or cloud storage for Game data.
  • Clearing the Game's cache or uninstalling the Game will remove all locally stored Game data.

5. Third-Party Services

  • App Store Privacy: Your in-app purchases are subject to your app store's privacy policy (Apple or Google). Please review their privacy policies for information on how they handle payment data.
  • No Other Third Parties: The Game does not share information with any other third-party services, advertisers, or analytics providers.

6. Children's Privacy

The Game does not knowingly collect any information from children or conduct targeted marketing to children. As the Game processes all information locally on the device, standard children's privacy protections apply as governed by your device's operating system.

If you have concerns about a child's privacy while using the Game, please contact us as detailed in Section 7.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

privacy@potatomotato.com

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

Privacy Commitment Summary

  • No data collection from gameplay
  • No server transmission of any information
  • No ads or advertising tracking
  • All processing is local to your device
  • In-app purchases only for additional players
  • Complete offline gameplay with local multiplayer via Bluetooth/WiFi
