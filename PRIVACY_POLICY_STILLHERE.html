Privacy Policy for Still Here

Last Updated: March 2, 2026 (v1)

1. Introduction

Still Here ("the App") is a welfare-check application that helps friends and family stay connected during periods of inactivity. Because the App collects sensitive data to fulfil this safety purpose, we take privacy extremely seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, how we protect it, and when we delete it.

Key principle: All sensitive sensor data (location, Bluetooth, WiFi, call log, device state) is encrypted on your device before it leaves your phone. We cannot read your encrypted data on our servers.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used for authentication and account recovery.

We do not collect your name, phone number, physical address, or government-issued identifiers.

2.2 Sensor Data (Heartbeats)

Every 60 minutes, the App collects the following data and bundles it into an encrypted "heartbeat":

Data Type What We Collect Why
Location GPS coordinates, accuracy, altitude, speed, bearing Show your last known position during a welfare alert
Bluetooth devices Device names, MAC addresses, signal strength Help identify your surroundings if location is unavailable
WiFi networks Network names (SSID), access-point addresses (BSSID), signal strength, frequency Provide environmental context during a welfare alert
Call log Last 10 calls: phone numbers, timestamps, duration, direction Help your emergency contacts understand your recent activity
Device state Battery level, charging status, connection type, signal strength Determine whether your phone is reachable
Encryption: All sensor data above is encrypted with AES-256-GCM on your device before upload. The encryption key is generated and stored locally. Only you and friends you have explicitly added can decrypt this data.

2.3 Friendship & Alert Data

  • Friendships: We store which users are connected, escalation priority order, and whether monitoring is active in each direction. No personal details beyond user IDs.
  • Alerts: When a welfare alert is triggered (by inactivity timeout or manually), a copy of your encrypted heartbeat history is attached to the alert so your friends can view your recent whereabouts. When the alert is resolved, the alert document and all attached data are permanently deleted. No alert history is retained.
  • Invitations: Invite links contain a cryptographic token. Invites expire after 7 days and can be revoked by the creator.

2.5 What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, physical address, or government ID.
  • We do not display advertisements or collect advertising identifiers.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to any third party.
  • We do not read the content of your messages, emails, or other communications.

3. How We Protect Your Data

3.1 End-to-End Encryption

All heartbeat sensor data is encrypted on your device using AES-256-GCM before being uploaded. The symmetric encryption key is stored in your device's hardware-backed Android KeyStore and never leaves your phone in plaintext.

When you add a friend, your encryption key is wrapped using ECIES (Elliptic Curve Integrated Encryption Scheme with P-256) so that only that specific friend can decrypt your heartbeat data. We (the developers) cannot decrypt your sensor data on our servers.

3.2 Local Key Storage

Authentication tokens and encryption keys stored on your device are protected with AES-256-GCM EncryptedSharedPreferences, backed by the Android KeyStore. This data is not included in device backups.

3.3 Server-Side Protections

  • Firebase App Check (Play Integrity) ensures only the genuine, unmodified App can access our backend services.
  • Firestore security rules restrict document access to authorised users only.
  • Tier and billing fields on your user profile can only be written by our server-side Cloud Functions, not by the App directly.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

4.1 Heartbeat History

Encrypted heartbeat snapshots are automatically pruned based on your subscription tier:

Tier Retention Window
Free6 hours
Standard24 hours
Premium48 hours

Heartbeats older than your tier's retention window are automatically deleted each time a new heartbeat is uploaded.

4.2 Alerts

When an alert is resolved (via check-in or dismissal), the alert document and all attached encrypted data are immediately and permanently deleted from our servers and from your device. No alert history is kept — resolved alerts cannot be retrieved.

4.3 Invitations

Invite tokens expire automatically after 7 days. Creators can revoke invites at any time.

4.4 Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the App's settings. Account deletion permanently removes:

  • Your user profile and authentication credentials
  • All friendship records in both directions
  • All alerts you are involved in
  • All pending invitations you created
  • Your heartbeat history
  • Your FCM push notification token

This action is irreversible. Local data (Room database, encrypted preferences) is cleared on the device where deletion is initiated.

5. In-App Purchases

  • The App offers optional subscription tiers (Standard and Premium) that increase friend limits and heartbeat history retention.
  • Purchases are processed by Google Play Billing and are subject to Google's privacy policy.
  • We do not receive or store your payment card details. We receive only a purchase token from Google Play, which we use to verify the transaction.
  • Subscriptions are not shared via Google Play Family Library.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all data the App holds about you from within the App (profile, friends, alerts, heartbeats).
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings.
  • Portability: Alert data can be exported as a text share-card from the alert detail screen.
  • Withdraw consent: You can revoke any device permission at any time through your device settings. The App will continue to function with reduced capability.
